About Us

Learn more about our mission, vision, and history

Our Story

Founded in 2010, EduTech Academy began with a simple mission: to make quality technology education accessible to everyone. What started as a small coding bootcamp has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving thousands of students worldwide.

Our founders, a group of technology professionals and educators, recognized the growing gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving tech industry. They created EduTech Academy to bridge this gap by offering practical, industry-relevant training programs.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with the knowledge, skills, and mindset needed to thrive in the digital economy through innovative, accessible, and practical technology education.

Our Vision

To be the leading global provider of technology education, recognized for excellence, innovation, and the success of our graduates in transforming the world through technology.

Our Core Values

Innovation

We embrace change and continuously seek new ways to improve our educational offerings and student experience.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from curriculum development to student support.

Inclusivity

We believe that technology education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or prior experience.

Community

We foster a supportive community where students, alumni, and instructors can connect, collaborate, and grow together.
