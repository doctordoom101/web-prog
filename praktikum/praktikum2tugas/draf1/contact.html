Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a program?

You can apply for any of our programs by filling out the application form on our website or by contacting our admissions team directly.

Are there any prerequisites for your programs?

Prerequisites vary by program. Some beginner programs have no prerequisites, while advanced programs may require prior knowledge or experience.

Do you offer financial aid or payment plans?

Yes, we offer various financial aid options and flexible payment plans to make our programs more accessible.

Can I visit the campus before applying?

We encourage prospective students to schedule a campus tour to meet our team and learn more about our facilities.
